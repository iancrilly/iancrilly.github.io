Does your panel have a 200A main breaker?

This is the largest breaker in your panel, both in size and on the number printed on it. Usually, it will say 100A, 125A, 150A, or 200A. The print on this breaker MUST be "200A" to continue.

In the example below, there are two styles of main breakers outlined in yellow. Notice how both are much larger than the rest. They also may have a sticker next to them labeled "Service Disconnect," shown in our example outlined in purple.

Would your charger be mounted within 5' or 25' of your panel?
